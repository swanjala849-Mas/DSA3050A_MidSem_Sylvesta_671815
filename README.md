## Project Overview

This project presents a business intelligence analysis of the **DataCo Supply Chain Dataset** using Microsoft Power BI and Excel. The project involves data cleaning and transformation using Power Query, interactive dashboard development, and business insights to support data-driven decision-making.

## Dataset Information

* **Dataset Source:** DataCo Supply Chain Dataset (Kaggle)
* **Dataset Link:** https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shashwatwork/dataco-smart-supply-chain-for-big-data-analysis 
* **Number of Rows:** 180,520
* **Number of Columns:** 53

## Business Problem

The objective of this project is to analyze supply chain operations in order to identify trends in sales, profit, customer behavior, product performance, and regional performance. The insights generated can help businesses improve operational efficiency, increase profitability, and make informed business decisions.

## Business Insights

### Insight 1

The Europe market generated the highest total profit, while Africa recorded the lowest profit. This indicates that Europe is the company's most profitable market and may present opportunities for further investment, whereas Africa may require strategies to improve profitability. 

### Insight 2

Most sales come from low-value orders. The "Sales by Order Value Category" chart shows that the Low order value category contributes the largest share of total sales, suggesting that the business relies heavily on a high volume of lower-value transactions rather than a smaller number of premium orders. 

### Insight 3

Standard Class is the most frequently used shipping mode. The shipping mode donut chart indicates that approximately 60% of total orders are shipped using Standard Class, making it the preferred shipping method among customers. This suggests the company should prioritize maintaining the efficiency and reliability of this shipping option.
